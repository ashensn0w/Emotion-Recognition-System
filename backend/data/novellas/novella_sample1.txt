Amidst the heavy rain, Clara ran swiftly toward the old house. The beating of her heart seemed louder than the raindrops. She was afraid of what might happen, but she couldn't ignore the letter she had received earlier. 'Come here, I have something important to tell you,' the note said. Despite the darkness and cold of the night, she pressed on. Her hands trembled as she touched the rusty doorknob. What could be waiting inside?
